      Things you should know

      • CSV exports may show transactions differently to those in your statements.
      • A closed account is a credit card, savings or transaction account that you no longer have access to. This includes cards that have been replaced due to fraud or have been lost or stolen.
      • You can export up to 3 years of closed account transactions for eligible personal Card Acccounts, Altitude Business and Business Choice cards. Closed account exports are only available to the primary card holder.
      • You can also view or download up to 7 years of eligible closed account statements if you need to access older transactions.
